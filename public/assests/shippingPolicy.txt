1) Order Processing Time


Orders are processed within 1-2 business days of receiving payment confirmation.
Processing times may vary during peak periods or due to high demand.




2) Shipping Methods and Delivery Times


We offer multiple shipping options, including standard and expedited shipping.
Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days.
Expedited shipping typically takes 1-3 business days.
Delivery times may vary based on the destination and shipping method chosen.


3) Shipping Costs


Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the chosen shipping method and delivery location.
We occasionally offer free shipping promotions, which will be clearly advertised on our website and app.


4) International Shipping


Currently, we only ship within India. We do not offer international shipping.
5. Tracking Orders


Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information.
You can track your order using the tracking number provided via the shipping carrier’s website.


6) Shipping Address


Please ensure your shipping address is accurate and complete at the time of order.
Refurbia is not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect or incomplete addresses.


7) Delivery Issues


If you do not receive your order within the expected delivery timeframe, please contact our customer service team at support@refurbia.in.
In case of a lost or stolen package, we will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue.


8) Damaged Items


If your order arrives damaged, please contact us immediately at support@refurbia.in with your order number and photos of the damaged item(s).
We will arrange for a return and replacement or refund based on the situation.
