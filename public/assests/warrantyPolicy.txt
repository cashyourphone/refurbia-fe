1. Overview
Refurbia, a product of Eftychia Technologies Pvt Ltd, is committed to providing high-quality refurbished smartphones to our customers. All our devices undergo a rigorous refurbishment process to ensure they meet our quality standards. This warranty policy outlines the terms and conditions under which we offer warranty coverage for our refurbished smartphones.
2. Warranty Coverage
Refurbia offers a limited warranty of 6 months from the date of purchase for all refurbished smartphones. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use, subject to the following terms and conditions.
3. Warranty Terms
3.1 Eligibility
The warranty is valid only for the original purchaser of the device from Refurbia. Proof of purchase (receipt or order number) is required to claim warranty service.
3.2 Coverage Details
The warranty covers the following:
Defects in materials and workmanship
Malfunctioning of hardware components (e.g., battery, screen, camera, buttons)
3.3 Exclusions
The warranty does not cover:
Damage caused by misuse, abuse, or accidents (e.g., dropping the device, liquid damage)
Unauthorised modifications or repairs
Normal wear and tear (e.g., scratches, scuffs)
Software issues or third-party applications
4. Warranty Claim Process
4.1 Initiating a Claim
To initiate a warranty claim, please contact Refurbia customer support at support@refurbia.in. Provide your order number and a detailed description of the issue.
4.2 Inspection and Repair
Upon receiving your claim, we will inspect the device to determine the cause of the issue. If the issue is covered under warranty, we will repair or replace the device at no cost to you. The decision to repair or replace the device is at the sole discretion of Refurbia.
4.3 Shipping
Customers are responsible for shipping the device to Refurbia for inspection. If the device is found to be covered under warranty, Refurbia will cover the return shipping costs. If the issue is not covered, the customer is responsible for all shipping costs.
5. Limitations of Liability
Refurbia's liability is limited to the repair or replacement of the defective device. Under no circumstances shall Refurbia be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use of the device.
6. Governing Law
This warranty policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any disputes arising from this policy shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Mumbai.
7. Changes to the Warranty Policy
Refurbia reserves the right to modify this warranty policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website and will apply to devices purchased after the effective date of the change.
