No Returns Accepted


At Refurbia, customer satisfaction is our priority. However, we do not accept returns once a product has been received by the customer. Please inspect the product carefully at the time of delivery.


Refusal of Delivery


Customers have the option to refuse the delivery of the product at their doorstep if they are not satisfied with the product. If you choose to refuse the delivery, the following conditions apply:


●Full Refund: If you refuse the delivery, you will be eligible for a full refund to your original method of payment.


●Inspection at Delivery: Please ensure you inspect the product thoroughly before accepting it from the delivery agent.


Refund Process


If you refuse the delivery and are eligible for a refund, the process will be as follows:


1) Confirmation: The delivery agent will confirm the refusal and return the product to us.


2) Refund Initiation: Once we receive the returned product, we will initiate the refund process.


2) Refund Completion: The refund will be processed and credited to your original method of payment within 7 business days.


3) Non-Returnable Items
As we do not accept returns after the product has been received, please note that all sales are considered final upon acceptance of the delivery.


Contact Information


If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding our return and refund policy, please contact our customer service team at support@refurbia.in.
